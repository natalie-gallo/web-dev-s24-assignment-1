The Cactus Wren

The cactus wren (Campylorhynchus brunneicapillus) are birds that settle within cactus plants, specifically the saguaro and cholla in order to hide from predators. They build their nests using materials from plants and line them with their feathers. The cactus wren does not migrate and stays in their home all year long. Their diet usually consists of insects but they will sometimes eat plants. Since they don't fly very well, they will scavenge for food on the ground, lifting up rocks and eating whatever lies beneath them.
